Tim The Trashman the game

The game Tim The Trashman

This is the game Tim The Trashman. You can play this game as the hero Tim, who saves the world every day as a trash collector. However, the trash is now done with Tim capturing them all the time and has now rebelled. Combining all their power the trash gang managed to lock up Tim in an inescapable prison consisting of four levels. Now Tim needs your help to escape this prison. Your goal is to clean the world, escape all four levels and overcome anything that might try to stop you.
